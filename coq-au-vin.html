Coq au Vin

Coq au vin is a classic French dish that combines chicken with wine, mushrooms, and bacon. The dish is slowly cooked until the chicken is tender and the flavors have melded together. This dish is perfect for a special occasion or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the bacon until crispy.
  2. Remove the bacon and set aside.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. In the same skillet, brown the chicken on all sides.
  5. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  6. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until soft.
  7. Add the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  8. Slowly whisk in the wine and chicken broth until smooth.
  9. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until thickened.
  10. Return the chicken and bacon to the skillet.
  11. Add the mushrooms.
  12. Cover and simmer for 1 hour or until the chicken is tender.

Enjoy!
