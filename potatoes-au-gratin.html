Potatoes au Gratin

Potatoes au gratin is a simple dish that combines thinly sliced potatoes with cream and cheese. The dish is baked until the potatoes are tender and the top is golden brown and bubbling. This dish is perfect for a holiday meal or a special dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds potatoes
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Peel and slice the potatoes thinly.
  3. Mince the garlic and onion.
  4. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  5. Add the garlic and onion and cook until soft.
  6. Add the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Slowly whisk in the cream until smooth.
  8. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook until thickened.
  9. Season with salt and pepper.
  10. In a greased baking dish, layer the potatoes and cheese.
  11. Pour the cream mixture over the potatoes.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until the top is golden brown.

Enjoy!
